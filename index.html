Merbau Wood

Merbau is a popular hardwood known for its durability, resistance to decay, and rich brown color. Often used in flooring, decking, and outdoor furniture, Merbau has a high density and strength, making it an excellent choice for both indoor and outdoor projects.

The interlocking grain and natural oils of Merbau wood contribute to its stability and resistance to weathering. It's a versatile material that can complement various architectural styles, from rustic to modern designs.

Teak Wood

Teak is renowned for its high quality and durability, making it a highly sought-after material for furniture and construction. Known for its golden-brown color and high oil content, teak is resistant to termites and weathering.

Often used in high-end outdoor furniture and flooring, teak wood‚Äôs fine grain and strength make it ideal for both aesthetic and functional applications. It requires minimal maintenance and retains its appearance for years.
